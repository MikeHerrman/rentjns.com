About JNS Vacation Rentals

We find forgotten coastal gems and give them a little TLC—then share them with travelers who love the comfort of home on their adventures. Clear pricing, thoughtful amenities, and friendly local help are our north star.

Based in Hoquiam, we’re hands-on hosts who care about easy check-ins, tidy spaces, and simple, memorable stays.

See our rentals

Our Story

It started with one small fixer on a quiet street around the corner from Quinault Casino. We learned what guests really use (good beds, a stocked kitchen, fast Wi-Fi) and what just gets in the way. Today, that same approach guides every place we open: practical comfort with a coastal calm.

We keep renovations light-touch and respectful—refresh, don’t erase—so each home keeps its character and fits right into the neighborhood.
